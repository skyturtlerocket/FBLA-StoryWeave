About

StoryWeave aims to be a platform for hosting interactive stories and to bring back interactive stories as a form of reading and entertainment. StoryWeave was made using Node.JS, Express, Rollup, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JSON. The logo and favicon was made with Looka.
